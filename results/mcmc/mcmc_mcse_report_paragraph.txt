Monte Carlo error was assessed with coda time-series standard errors on saved baseline draws for sigma, sigma_alpha, mu_alpha, and beta[1]-beta[3] (Table/Figure: mcmc_mcse_summary). MCSE-to-posterior-SD ratios ranged from about 0.013 to 0.039, with ESS roughly 640-6,300. Sampling uncertainty in posterior means was small relative to posterior spread, so reported summaries, model comparison, and prediction used the converged chains.
